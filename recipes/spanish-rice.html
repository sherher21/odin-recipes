Spanish Rice

Description

This is my second time making this dish so I'm not sure if it is correct or not but I'm recalling the recipe based on how I recently made it. The recipe can definitely be improved but I haven't done enough research and had enough Spanish Rice to know what is good and what is better. However, I still think this recipe will suffice for most palettes.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Chicken Bouillon

Directions

  1. Prepare Ingredients
    • Wash rice thoroughly and drain.
    • Peel garlic and onion.
    • Cut onion and tomato into smaller chucks.
    • Use a blender to blend onion tomato, onion, and garlic.
    • Add water to dilute and continue to blend thoroughly.
  2. Toast rice in pan with oil.
  3. After rice has toasted and is starting to brown, add the blended mixture with the rice.
  4. Mix the rice and onion-tomato mixture thoroughly.
  5. If it looks like there's not enough water, add water to the rice.
  6. Once the rice has started to boil, turn down the heat and cover with a lid or aluminum foil.
  7. Turn down the heat and let the rice slowly cook.
  8. When the rice is almost done cooking, turn off the heat and let the rice finish cooking.
  9. Scrape the bottom of the pan to prevent the rice from burning too much and to make cleaning the pan easier.
