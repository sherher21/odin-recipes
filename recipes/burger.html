Hamburger Recipe

Description

The picture is not what the burger will actually look like but the burger in the picture looks great anyway. This recipe will be my take on the classic cheese burger. The burger will be flavorful and filled with classic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Potato Buns
  • Beef Patties
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoe
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Thousand Island
  • Mustard

Directions

  1. Prepare Ingredients
    • Hand pull lettuce into smaller chucks.
    • Cut tomato into slices.
    • Cut onion into slices.
  2. Cook beef patties. Season the patties well on the grill.
  3. Add mustard and cheese onto the patty while the patty is still on the grill.
  4. Toast bun in toaster or toast on grill/pan with butter.
  5. After the bun has been toasted, spread Thousand Island onto both sides of the bun.
  6. On the bottom bun, add tomato slices and lettuce.
  7. Next, add the patty with cheese.
  8. Add sliced onions on top of the patty cheese.
  9. You can add another patty cheese on top of the onion if you have more
  10. Finish the burger by placing the top bun over the patty cheese/sliced onion
